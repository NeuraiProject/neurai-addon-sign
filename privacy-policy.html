Privacy Policy

Product: Neurai Sign
Last Updated: February 27, 2026

Neurai Sign is a browser extension used to manage Neurai (XNA) wallet actions and sign messages for authentication. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how it is used.

1. Data We Process

Neurai Sign may process the following data inside your browser:

  • Wallet address and public key
  • Encrypted private key data
  • PIN hash and lock settings
  • Selected network and optional RPC endpoint settings
  • Pending signing request details (for approval UI)

2. Where Data Is Stored

Extension data is stored locally using Chrome extension storage (chrome.storage.local). Private keys are encrypted locally with your PIN.

3. How Data Is Used

  • To show wallet information and balances
  • To allow message signing after explicit user approval
  • To protect access with PIN and auto-lock controls
  • To remember extension preferences across sessions

4. Data Sharing

Neurai Sign does not sell your personal data. Private keys are not transmitted to external services by the extension.

5. Network Requests

The extension sends RPC requests to Neurai blockchain endpoints (or user-configured RPC endpoints) to fetch blockchain data and verify signatures when needed.

6. User Control

You can control your data at any time by:

  • Removing accounts from the extension
  • Changing or removing your PIN
  • Uninstalling the extension (which removes extension storage)

7. Security

We apply local security measures such as PIN-based encryption and session auto-lock. However, you are responsible for protecting your device and keeping your PIN confidential.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the extension evolves. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last Updated” date on this page.

9. Contact

If you have privacy questions, you can contact us at dev@neurai.org or visit https://neurai.org.
